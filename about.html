公司简介 Company Introduction

佛山市中普铝业有限公司成立于2020年，是一家新兴的专业从事铝型材挤压生产的高新技术企业。公司位于广东省佛山市南海区，地理位置优越，交通便利。 Foshan Zhongpu Aluminum Co., Ltd. was established in 2020 and is a newly emerging high-tech enterprise specializing in aluminum profile extrusion production. The company is located in Nanhai District, Foshan City, Guangdong Province, with excellent geographical location and convenient transportation.

经过多年的发展，公司已建立了完善的生产体系和质量管理系统，拥有多条先进的挤压生产线，年生产能力达到10,000吨。我们的产品广泛应用于建筑、工业、装饰、交通运输等领域。 After years of development, the company has established a complete production and quality management system, with multiple advanced extrusion production lines and an annual production capacity of 10,000 tons. Our products are widely used in construction, industry, decoration, transportation and other fields.

公司始终坚持"质量第一、客户至上、诚信经营、持续创新"的经营理念，致力于为客户提供高品质、多样化的铝型材产品和专业的技术服务。 The company always adheres to the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Foremost, Integrity Management, Continuous Innovation", and is committed to providing customers with high-quality, diversified aluminum profile products and professional technical services.
