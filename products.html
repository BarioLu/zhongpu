Industrial Aluminum Profile

High-performance industrial aluminum profiles reshape the future of lightweight intelligent manufacturing. Our profiles are made from high-quality aluminum alloys and formed by precision extrusion, featuring three major advantages: lightweight and high strength (only 1/3 the density of steel, outstanding load capacity), excellent corrosion resistance (natural oxide film resists harsh environments), and flexible assembly (modular slot design, quick installation without welding). Custom surface treatments and special cross-sections are supported. From automation line frames to new energy heat dissipation components, we provide reliable structural support and innovative possibilities for the industrial field with green, cost-effective solutions.

  • High-precision extrusion, small tolerance, good interchangeability
  • High strength and load capacity, suitable for heavy-duty structures
  • Anodized surface, corrosion and wear resistant
  • Modular design, rich accessories, easy to assemble and disassemble
  • Custom mold opening supported for special industrial requirements

Typical Applications

  • Automation equipment frames, machine safety fences
  • Assembly lines, workbenches, material racks
  • Exhibition displays, inspection fixtures, lab platforms
  • Logistics conveyors, warehouse racks, etc.
