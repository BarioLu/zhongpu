关于中普铝业 About Zhongpu Aluminum

佛山市中普铝业有限公司是一家专业从事铝型材挤压生产的企业，拥有先进的生产设备和专业的技术团队。我们致力于为客户提供高品质、多样化的铝型材产品，广泛应用于建筑、工业、装饰等领域。 Foshan Zhongpu Aluminum Co., Ltd. is a professional enterprise engaged in aluminum profile extrusion production, with advanced production equipment and a professional technical team. We are committed to providing customers with high-quality, diversified aluminum profile products, widely used in construction, industry, decoration and other fields.

公司秉承"质量第一、客户至上"的经营理念，通过持续的技术创新和品质管理，赢得了广大客户的信赖和支持。 Adhering to the business philosophy of "Quality First, Customer Foremost", the company has won the trust and support of our customers through continuous technological innovation and quality management.
